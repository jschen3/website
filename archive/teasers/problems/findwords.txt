Find Words
April 24,2016
04 24 2016
A simple finding words problem. It can be done easily with a set of loops
Loops, standard in, standard out
Given a stream of characters (e.g. acacabcatghhellomvnsdb) and a list of words (e.g.["aca","cat","hello","world"] ) find and display count of each and every word once the stream ends. The stream of characters and list of words will be given through standard in and standard out. In the format:
1
acacabcatghhellomvnsdb
aca,cat,hello,world
The 1 is the number of trials
The second line is the main string
the third line is list of words to find.
